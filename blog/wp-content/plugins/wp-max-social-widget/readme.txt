== Description ==

WP Max Social Wigdet : wordpress Social widget having most of the social icon and social bookmarking option .Wp max social widget reside on the sidebar of the wordpress.having social links like : g+,facebook,pinterest,twitter,news feed subscription,dribbble,vimeo,digg,stumbleupon and more.
